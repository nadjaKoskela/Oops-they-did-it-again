Lizzie Borden

A gruesome crime and children's rhyme

Lizzie Borden’s story is without a question unique: She managed to escape justice, although it is more than probable that she committed what she was accused of in the end of the 19th century. She certainly remains a legend until this day, with kids still singing the children’s rhyme about her gruesome crimes: Lizzie Borden took an axe and gave her mother forty whacks. When she saw what she had done, she gave her father forty-one...
